About Wintria


We love news. Whether it’s sports, politics or fashion, we believe information should be easily discovered and unified. That’s why we built Wintria.

Wintria is a search engine exclusively for news. You can now see news from a variety of news sources all in one place.

Traditionally, news has been organized into categories. We’re changing the way you experience news by bringing keyword search to the forefront in an intuitive and interactive interface.

Our goal is to become the news content leader in providing an engaging search experience. Interested in what’s in store for Wintria 2.0? Sign up now to receive your exclusive invite.
